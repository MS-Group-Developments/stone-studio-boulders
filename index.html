What we do

Stone that reads
the landscape
and marks it.

A boulder is the oldest form of marker. We take that instinct and apply six generations of stonemasonry to it — hand-selecting every stone, cutting the lettering by hand or by photo-blast, and installing to the tolerances an architect or landscape designer expects.

We work with housebuilders, landscape architects, estate owners and public realm specifiers. Our commissions range from single development entrance boulders to multi-stone wayfinding trails and public art installations.

Supply-only or full install. Foundation co-ordination included where required. British quarried stone by default — we can source internationally where the specification requires.

Services

What we make.

01

Development Entrance Boulders

Named entrance features for residential and commercial developments. Development name, logo or monogram. Glacial or quarried boulders. Installation co-ordinated with site programme.

02

Monolith Signage

Upright monoliths in Welsh slate, granite or sandstone. Wayfinding, naming and interpretive content. Clean architectural lines — for settings where a boulder would read as informal.

03

Public Art & Trails

Multi-stone art installations and wayfinding trails for parks, public realm and heritage sites. We work to the artist's or landscape architect's brief — fabrication and installation as a single service.

04

Estate & Rural Features

Entrance features, boundary markers and estate identification for private rural estates. Welsh granite, Scottish granite, Yorkshire sandstone — matched to the landscape.

How it works

From field to fixing.

01

Stone Selection

We hand-pick from quarry or select from our yard — matching size, character, colour and grain to your setting and brief.

02

Design & Proof

Artwork, lettering layout and finish options presented for approval. Scale drawings supplied. Working with your landscape architect throughout.

03

Cutting & Finishing

Hand-cut or photo-blasted lettering. Painted, gilded or natural finish. Every piece finished in our Cardiff workshop.

04

Foundation Design

We specify or co-ordinate the foundation — carbon steel fixing, concrete base, or set-in-ground depending on weight and permanence requirement.

05

Install

Our team or supply-only to your groundwork contractor. Site survey, lifting, final fixing and sign-off. UK-wide.
