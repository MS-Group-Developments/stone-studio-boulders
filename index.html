Boulders

Each boulder is chosen, not made.

We source, select and place natural boulders for landscape architects, garden designers and private clients. From a single focal stone to a site-wide material palette.

CNC Circular Saw

Each boulder is trimmed to a flat base using our CNC circular saw вЂ” lubricated by a closed-loop water system that recycles over 95% of water used.

Sandblasting

A rubber template of your design is applied to the face of the boulder. High-pressure abrasive sandblasting then inscribes your text, logo or pattern into the natural stone surface.
